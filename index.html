Professional Background

Dr. Mohamed Hasain N holds a doctorate in Transportation Engineering from the National Institute of Technology Silchar, India. His research interests include traffic safety analysis, crash modelling, transportation planning, and direct demand modelling using machine learning techniques. He was awarded the University Silver Medal for academic excellence in his master's program at NIT Silchar. Dr. Hasain is currently serving as a Post-Doctoral Researcher at the Transportation Systems Engineering Laboratory, Department of Civil Engineering, Indian Institute of Technology Bombay. He has been actively involved in several research projects aimed at improving safe and secure first- and last-mile connectivity, particularly for pedestrians, in metro transit systems, as well as assessing the resilience of suburban rail networks in emerging countries in response to rainfall events.
